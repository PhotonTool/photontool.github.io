About Us

Photon Fund, established in 2017 by a team of seasoned venture capital professionals, is dedicated to consistently identifying and supporting world-class technology teams, striving to help them grow into leading global technology companies.

Investment Strategy

Photon Fund specializes in deep-tech investments, focusing on innovative startups in cutting-edge technology and biotech. We adhere to an investment philosophy of selective, prudent, and substantial investments. By continuously tracking and deeply understanding frontier technologies, we make early-stage investments in technologies or startups that demonstrate breakthrough innovations and significant application potential. Photon Fund strives to invest before a startup's value inflection point and increases investment when industry trends become clear and the market enters a phase of rapid growth, aiming to maximize investment returns.
